~ About Department ~
⚙

The Mechanical Engineering Department deals with design, analysis, manufacturing and maintenance of mechanical systems. The department emphasizes strong fundamentals, hands-on training and industrial exposure.

👁

Vision

To produce competent mechanical engineers with innovative thinking, technical expertise and ethical values to meet industry and societal needs.

🎯

Mission

  • Provide quality education in mechanical engineering.
  • Enhance practical skills through workshops and labs.
  • Encourage innovation, projects and industry interaction.
  • Develop teamwork, leadership and ethical responsibility.
🎓

Program Outcomes (POs)

  • Apply mechanical engineering principles effectively.
  • Design and analyze mechanical components and systems.
  • Use modern tools, machines and software.
  • Work efficiently in multidisciplinary teams.
  • Adapt to emerging technologies and lifelong learning.
📘

What Students Learn

  • Engineering Mechanics and Strength of Materials
  • Thermodynamics and Heat Power Engineering
  • Manufacturing Processes and CNC Machines
  • CAD / CAM and Industrial Design
  • Automobile Engineering Basics

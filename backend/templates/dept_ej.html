~ About Department ~
📡

The Electronics & Telecommunication Engineering Department deals with design, development and maintenance of electronic and communication systems. The department focuses on both hardware and software aspects of modern communication technologies.

👁

Vision

To create technically skilled and innovative electronics engineers capable of contributing to communication and electronics industries.

🎯

Mission

  • Provide strong foundation in electronics and communication.
  • Enhance practical skills through labs and projects.
  • Encourage innovation, research and industry exposure.
  • Develop ethical values and professional competence.
🎓

Program Outcomes (POs)

  • Design and analyze electronic circuits.
  • Apply communication engineering principles.
  • Use modern tools and simulation software.
  • Work effectively in teams with ethics.
  • Adapt to emerging communication technologies.
📘

What Students Learn

  • Analog & Digital Electronics
  • Communication Systems
  • Microcontrollers & Embedded Systems
  • Wireless & Optical Communication
  • PCB Design and Electronic Testing
